Introduction

Decision trees are widely used models for classification and regression tasks. Essentially, they learn a hierarchy of if/else questions, leading to a decision.

Usually, a decision tree selects the optimal features recursively, then splits data sets based on such optimal features. The goal is to have a better data classification procedure.
